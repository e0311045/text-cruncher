About Us

The idea was born from the many hours of frustration researching for valid and reliable data. As time is limited, we seek to eliminate this certain negativity in our day to day life. We strongly feel that this could be the big step forward for many search engines but with advertising money on the line, many may not feel the need to over optimize searches. So here we are with no advertising money, limited computing resources and a school project to finish, we present to you TextCruncher!

TextCruncher scrapes the web and basically generate a data file with all the needed information you need. The data is as relevant and valid as google could provide us. Just enjoy using this all convenient program.

The mechanics at work is a wonder, many packages have been developed by established organizations and programmers. One package that will be heavily utilize over the years would be “tensorflow”. This package focuses on improving the structure of the program “brain”. The program is currently running of tensorflow 1.8 version and we are still looking forward to integrate the source codes to run on tensorflow 2.0, the newest version at as the time of writing.
